Go Label Feature allows you to register sign language hand movement as a dataset then will be used as following:

  • User can register N-number of sequences for each ASL word.
  • Current version, 20 sequences can be registered for each ASL word.
  • Collected data is auto-saved.
  • No data is collected such as name, photos, etc.
  • After collecting gestures is complete, user can train their dataset.
  • Click "Start Labeling", your camera will open and start providing gestures.
  • Available words are: Peace, Like, Dislike and ok.
  • When labeling is done, scroll down to train your dataset.
