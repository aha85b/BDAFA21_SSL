About us

Our goal is to bridge the gap between deaf and normal people by allowing users to build their dataset, label it, and train an appropriate model on our website and get the prediction of their gestures.

What our aim

The aim of this web application is to build a model to recognize American Sign Language (ASL) using computer vision and Google MediaPipe in real time. We are using Media-pipe tool in order to track the user fingers and hand gestures to predict the word.
